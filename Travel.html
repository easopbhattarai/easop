Travel Experiences


Bouddhanath Stupa

Bouddhanath Stupa is very close to my heart, and also located near my home in Kathmandu, Nepal. It is the largest Buddhist Stupa in the World and is also protected as a UNESCO World Heritage Site. I used to go there every single day, alone, or with friends.

North Myrtle Beach, SC

This is the first time I ever saw the ocean; first time I went to the beach. Spring break, freshman year of college, right before the coronavirus pandemic, I had one of the most exciting experiences of my life. As a kid who grew up in a valley of a landlocked mountaneous country, getting to the beach was one of my top items in my bucket list.

Cincinnati, OH

The first major city I visited after coming to The United States. It was only a couple of days since I landed in Kentucky for college, when I got to go to Cincinnati with the other Internaitonal Students. It was the day I first felt, "Wow, I am really in America".

Falmouth, KY

My first trip since arriving to the US. The International Office, NKU took students up to Camp Northward in Falmouth,KY for fall break. I got to see some rural areas of Kentucky where I saw people farming, horses running and grazing, housing that I had only seen in the movies and many new things. We stayed in a treehouse, ziplined, went to BLINK Cincinnati, too.
